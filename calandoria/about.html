What we stand for

Our values

Belonging over metrics
We don't optimize for engagement or time-on-platform. We optimize for making you feel welcome. Those are different things.
Quality over quantity
Every server we run is maintained, moderated, and improved. We'd rather have fewer, better worlds than hundreds of neglected ones.
Transparency
We publish our moderation policies, hold public Q&As, and never make major changes without community consultation first.
Learning through play
From economy simulations to collaborative building, our worlds are designed to teach real skills in ways that never feel like work.
Inclusivity
Calandoria welcomes players of all ages, backgrounds, and skill levels. Our code of conduct is enforced consistently, without exceptions.
Sustainability
We run on renewable energy, pay our moderators, and reinvest revenue directly into server infrastructure and community programs.
